History

TechCon was founded in 2012 as a small meetup for local tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has evolved into a global platform bringing together top minds in technology, innovation, and business. The conference has grown to include thousands of attendees and hundreds of industry-leading speakers from around the world.

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire, connect, and empower individuals who are passionate about shaping the future of technology. We believe in fostering innovation through collaboration and sharing knowledge to solve real-world problems.

Past Speakers

Dr. Maya Roberts

Renowned AI researcher and TED speaker, Dr. Roberts has led groundbreaking work in ethical machine learning. Her talk on “AI for Humanity” in 2020 inspired industry-wide discussions.

Linda Chen

As CTO of NetBridge, Linda spoke in 2023 about quantum networking and the next era of internet connectivity. Her research continues to influence cloud and IoT architectures worldwide.
